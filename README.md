Cyber-Bio Dojo
AI-Powered Human Performance Laboratory
Overview

Cyber-Bio Dojo is an open research platform that combines embedded systems, wearable biosensors, artificial intelligence, and modular training equipment into a unified human performance laboratory.

The project explores how AI and cyber-physical systems can assist with training by collecting real-time physiological, biomechanical, and environmental data to help users understand their performance, monitor progress, and support informed training decisions.

Built around modular 2020 aluminum extrusion, the platform integrates smart exercise equipment, wearable devices, and an AI assessment kiosk capable of monitoring body metrics, tracking workouts, and visualizing performance over time.

Rather than functioning as a traditional gym, Cyber-Bio Dojo serves as a research environment where every workout contributes data toward developing adaptive training systems, digital twin models, and human performance analytics.
